# Country and Channel Concept

Last updated: 2026-03-04

1. One global operating model, with local channel differences.
2. Some markets are email-first; some are phone/WhatsApp-first.
3. Loyalty signup and campaign response improve when local habits are respected.
4. Global team sets standards; local teams tune messaging and channel mix.

Non-negotiable rule:
1. Capture and store consent per channel (email, SMS, WhatsApp).
