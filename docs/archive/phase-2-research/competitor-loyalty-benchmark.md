# Competitor Loyalty Benchmark (Quick)

Last updated: 2026-03-03

## What similar parks commonly do

1. Sell recurring monthly/annual passes.
2. Offer clear member perks (discounts, guest passes, priority access).
3. Tie perks to digital account/check-in instead of paper cards.
4. Adjust benefits by location in franchise setups.

## Examples reviewed

1. Sky Zone - membership and rewards focus  
   https://www.skyzone.com/membership
2. Urban Air - tiered annual pass model  
   https://www.urbanair.com/annual-passes/
3. Altitude - franchise-by-location membership pages  
   https://www.altitudetrampolinepark.com/activities-and-attractions/membership/
4. Launch - multiple pass products  
   https://launchentertainmentpark.com/pass/

## What this means for park

1. Keep loyalty digital-first (QR/account), not paper-first.
2. Keep rewards simple and visible.
3. Allow country/franchise tuning inside one global model.
