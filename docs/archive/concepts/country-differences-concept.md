# Country Differences Concept

Last updated: 2026-03-04

## Core idea

1. Use one global operating model.
2. Allow local market differences in channels, language, and legal requirements.
3. Keep central standards for data quality, reporting, and loyalty rules.

## Differences to plan for

1. Identity preference:
   - Some markets use email first.
   - Some markets prefer phone/WhatsApp.
2. Consent and privacy:
   - Consent text and legal basis vary by country.
3. Communication channels:
   - Email may work best in one market, SMS/WhatsApp in another.
4. Campaign style and offers:
   - Promotions and wording need local fit.

## Minimum market checklist

1. Supported identity method (email, phone, both).
2. Allowed marketing channels and consent flows.
3. Local owner for legal/compliance sign-off.
4. Localized templates for messages and loyalty promotion.
5. KPI baseline for that market (conversion, repeat rate, loyalty sign-up).

## Governance model

1. Global team defines platform standards.
2. Country teams choose local channel mix and messaging within standards.
3. Review results monthly and share best practices across markets.
