# Proposition: Visit-Based Marketing (No Loyalty Points UI)

Last updated: 2026-03-18

## Summary

Do not launch stamps or points initially. Use visit and purchase behavior to trigger offers and retention messaging.

## How loyalty works

1. Visitor registers account/contact details.
2. ROLLER tracks visits and purchases.
3. Marketing platform sends campaigns by visit stage (first visit, return prompt, win-back).
4. Incentives are delivered as offers, vouchers, or targeted discounts.

## How visitor data is captured

1. Sign-in/account creation captures email or phone.
2. Purchases, bookings, and behavior events are tracked.
3. Marketing platform uses segments and automation triggers.

## Why choose this

1. Fastest and simplest path.
2. No custom loyalty mechanics to maintain.
3. Immediate marketing value from captured data.

## Risks

1. No visible progress mechanic may reduce emotional engagement.
2. Harder to communicate a clear long-term reward journey.
3. Requires strong campaign discipline to stay effective.

## Decision fit

Choose this if the primary launch objective is data capture and lifecycle marketing, not gamified loyalty.
