# Proposition: ROLLER Membership + Marketing Automation

Last updated: 2026-03-18

## Summary

Use ROLLER native membership and account features now, then drive repeat visits with targeted marketing automations. Do not implement stamp UI in this option.

## How loyalty works

1. Visitor creates/signs in to ROLLER account.
2. Visitor gets membership benefits that are already supported.
3. Repeat-visit behavior is encouraged via email/SMS campaigns and offers.

## How visitor data is captured

1. Online account sign-in captures email or phone identity.
2. Booking and purchase events are stored in ROLLER.
3. Tracking events can be sent to marketing tools via integrations/API.

## Why choose this

1. Lowest delivery risk using current documented ROLLER features.
2. No custom loyalty UI to build or maintain.
3. Good base for marketing segmentation and lifecycle messaging.

## Risks

1. No visible stamp/points progress for visitor.
2. May feel less engaging than game-like loyalty.
3. Native points timeline from ROLLER is still not publicly documented.

## Decision fit

Choose this if reliability and speed matter more than a visual stamp journey.
